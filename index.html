VANYARD BEGINNING

In January 2023, Vanyard, a renowned culinary establishment, opened its doors in Zamboanga City, Philippines, captivating locals with its commitment to exceptional dining experiences. Among its diverse menu, the Chicken Inasal emerged as a standout favorite, showcasing Vanyard's dedication to authentic flavors and culinary excellence. Grilled to perfection over an open flame, each succulent skewer of marinated chicken, served with fragrant rice and traditional dipping sauces, captivated diners' palates and became the restaurant's signature dish. As word of Vanyard's culinary prowess spread, it quickly became a beloved fixture in Zamboanga's vibrant food scene, attracting enthusiasts from across the region to savor its unforgettable flavors.

OUR RESTAURANT

Embark on a sensory journey through the lively vanyards, where every step offers a glimpse into the heart and soul of Filipino culture. Amidst the bustling atmosphere, you'll encounter a myriad of sights, sounds, and flavors that ignite the senses. From sizzling street food stalls serving up local delicacies to vibrant shops showcasing colorful goods, the vanyards are a treasure trove of experiences waiting to be discovered. Lose yourself in the vibrant energy and rich tapestry of this dynamic community, where every corner tells a story and every moment is a celebration of life.
